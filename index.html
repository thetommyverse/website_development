Tommy Hills BSc.

Tommy Hills is a passionate STEM communicator, based in Milton Keynes, with his blend of Mathematics, Computer Science, and Education seamlessly intertwining with the world of performing and creative arts. Currently, Tommy wears multiple hats as a dedicated Masters of Science by Research student at the University of Kent, Teacher of Computing through the Teach First Training Programme, enthusiastic STEM communicator and moonlights as a PowerPoint comedian. Tommy's love of stand-up comedy, working with artificial intelligence and exploring the history and nerdy quirks of our mathematical world, culminate in fun and engaging talks and performances, designed to show the full extent of creativity within Computing. What sets Tommy apart is his deep appreciation for performance, historical insights, and the fascinating quirks of the mathematical and computational universe. His diverse interests come together to create captivating talks and performances that are both enjoyable and educational. The mission is to demonstrate that mathematics and computer science extend far beyond the confines of the classroom, proving that they can be both fun and incredibly useful in our everyday lives.

About

Research

Tommy is a dedicated, determined and dynamic researcher at the University of Kent, delving into the creative potential of LLMs (Large Language Models); investigating whether these advanced AI models, such as Claude 3, Google Gemini and GPT-4 can prove adept at writing stand-up comedy material. Tommy's work has not gone unnoticed, getting involved in the world of public engagement, sharing insights about their research in various forums. Tommy has worked with BBC News South East, and BBC Radio 1 with consultation about the applications of Generative AI in various places. Tommy is not just a researcher; they are a passionate advocate for the dissemination of knowledge and information. They have taken the stage to deliver captivating talks and STEM presentations, aiming to enlighten, educate, and showcase the intriguing intersection of artificial intelligence and comedy. Performance



Tommy is not only an academic but also a seasoned performer with a passion for captivating audiences through the art of public speaking, stand-up comedy, and spoken-word poetry. Within the realm of stand-up comedy, Tommy has carved out a distinctive niche with their unique style. Known for their love of character comedy infused with the creative use of PowerPoint presentations, earning them the well-deserved moniker of "PPT, the PowerPoint Tommy." This innovative approach adds a dynamic and educational twist to their comedy, delighting audiences with both humour and insightful visuals.


Tommy's unique comedic style and commitment to entertaining while educating set them apart as a multifaceted performer and academic, making them a standout in both worlds.
